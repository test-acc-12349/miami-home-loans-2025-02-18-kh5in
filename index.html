Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for a mortgage application?

For a standard application, you'll need: recent pay stubs (last 2 months), W-2s and tax returns (last 2 years), bank statements (last 2 months), government-issued ID, and proof of assets. Self-employed applicants may need additional documentation. We'll provide a detailed checklist based on your situation.

How long does the entire mortgage process take?

Typically, from application to closing takes 30-45 days. However, our expedited process can complete in as little as 21 days for well-prepared borrowers. Pre-approval can be completed in 24-48 hours.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

While we offer programs for various credit profiles, most conventional loans require a minimum score of 620. FHA loans may accept scores as low as 580. We can help you understand your options regardless of your current score.

How much down payment do I need?

Down payment requirements vary by loan type: Conventional loans typically require 5-20%, FHA loans as low as 3.5%, and VA loans may require no down payment. We also offer special programs for first-time buyers with down payments as low as 3%.

Can I get a mortgage if I'm self-employed?

Yes! We specialize in self-employed mortgages. While you'll need to provide additional documentation (typically 2 years of tax returns and business financials), our expertise in this area helps streamline the process for business owners and freelancers.
